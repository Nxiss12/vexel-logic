Partner Program FAQ

How much do I earn per client?

20% of their monthly subscription. If they pay £249/mo, you earn £49.80/mo per client, paid monthly as long as they remain a customer.

Do I need to do any technical setup?

No. You sell, we setup. We handle all onboarding, training, and technical configuration for your clients.

Is this white label?

Yes. Your branding, your logo, your domain. Your clients never know Vexel Logic exists. It's YOUR product.

What if my client needs support?

We provide white label support. Clients email YOUR support address (e.g., support@youragency.com), which forwards to us. We respond as YOUR team.

Is there exclusivity?

No. You're free to work with other partners, sell other products, whatever. We don't lock you in.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. If you stop referring clients, no problem. Your existing clients continue and you keep earning commission on them.

What if a client cancels?

You stop earning commission on that client. But if you've built a portfolio of 20+ clients, one cancellation won't hurt much.
