## Tips for Using Templates

**Personalize:**
- Always replace [Name], [Company], [Industry]
- Add specific details you know about them
- Reference recent conversations

**Timing:**
- Cold emails: Tue-Thu, 9am-11am
- Follow-ups: Wait 3-5 days
- Check-ins: Send monthly
- Support: Respond within 4 hours

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Ask questions when appropriate
- Be specific ("Quick question about [Company]")
- Avoid spam words ("Free", "Act now", "Limited time")
