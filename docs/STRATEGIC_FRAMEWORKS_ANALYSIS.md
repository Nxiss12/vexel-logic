- **Function:** Analyze historical interaction data to predict which leads will convert

##### 2. **Revenue Opportunity Identifier**
- **Function:** Surface upsell/cross-sell opportunities from customer data
- **Data inputs:** Purchase history, engagement patterns, industry benchmarks
- **Output:** "Customer X is ready for upgrade" or "Offer service Y to segment Z"
- **Use case:** "Customers who bought A also bought B within 30 days - proactively offer it"
- **Impact:** 15-25% revenue increase from existing customers

##### 3. **Churn Risk Detection**
- **Function:** Identify customers at risk of canceling BEFORE they leave
- **Indicators:** Decreased login frequency, reduced feature usage, support ticket sentiment
- **Output:** "High churn risk" alert with suggested intervention
- **Use case:** Auto-trigger retention campaign or personal outreach
- **Retention impact:** Reduce churn from 8% to <5% monthly

#### Phase 2: **Optimization Recommendation Engine** (Months 7-12)

##### 1. **Workflow Efficiency Analyzer**
- **Function:** Identify bottlenecks in customer workflows
- **Analysis:** Time-to-complete metrics, error rates, manual touchpoints
- **Output:** "Your invoicing process takes 12 min avg - automate X to reduce to 2 min"
- **Use case:** Continuous improvement suggestions delivered monthly
- **Time saving:** Additional 5-10 hours/week per customer

##### 2. **Pricing Optimization Algorithm**
- **Function:** Recommend optimal pricing based on market data and conversion rates
- **Data:** Industry benchmarks, competitor pricing, customer acceptance rates
- **Output:** "Increase service X price by 8% - minimal churn risk, ¬£14k annual revenue gain"
- **Use case:** Data-driven pricing decisions (vs. gut-feel)
