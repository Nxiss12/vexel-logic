### Partnership Success Factors

**What Works:**
1. **Aligned Incentives:** Partners make money when we make money (win-win)
2. **Low Barrier to Entry:** Sign up in 5 min, no upfront costs
3. **Strong Support:** Dedicated partner manager, quick responses
4. **Proven ROI:** Case studies show partners earning Â£1k-Â£15k/year
5. **Easy to Explain:** "Automation for SMEs" is simple pitch

**What Doesn't Work:**
1. **Complex Commission Structures:** Keep it simple (20% recurring, done)
2. **Slow Onboarding:** Partners lose interest if it takes >1 week to start
3. **Poor Communication:** Monthly newsletter minimum (stay top-of-mind)
4. **No Marketing Support:** Partners need collateral (don't make them create it)
