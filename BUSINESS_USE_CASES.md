## üí¨ Pro Tips for Power Users

1. **Be Specific:** Include numbers, dates, and context
   - ‚ùå "Find competitors"
   - ‚úÖ "Find top 5 competitors in [specific niche] with annual revenue > $10M"

2. **Use Session IDs:** Keep related conversations together
   - Example: Use project names as session IDs ("project-website-redesign")

3. **Chain Requests:** The agent remembers context
   - Message 1: "Research pricing for premium coffee subscriptions"
   - Message 2: "Now calculate what our pricing should be with a 40% margin"
   - Message 3: "Draft an email announcing our new pricing"

4. **Save Outputs:** Export responses to your notes/CRM

5. **Iterate:** If the output isn't perfect, refine your ask
   - "Make that email more casual"
   - "Recalculate with a 35% margin instead"
